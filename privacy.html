Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Preyer ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Preyer stores all data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Your prayer history and streak data
  • Your app settings and preferences
  • Your selected blocked apps (managed by Apple's Screen Time API)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Screen Time API

Preyer uses Apple's Family Controls and Screen Time API to manage app shielding. Apple designed these APIs with privacy in mind - the App cannot see which specific apps you have selected, only opaque tokens. App selection data is handled entirely by Apple's framework.

Advertising

The free version of Preyer displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google's privacy policy. You can remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools beyond what is provided by AdMob in the free version.

Children's Privacy

Preyer does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@preyerapp.com.
