Frequently Asked Questions

How do I block apps?

Go to Settings in the app, tap "Edit Blocked Apps", and select the apps you want to require a prayer before opening.

How does the prayer shield work?

When you open a blocked app, iOS shows a prayer screen. Complete the prayer to unlock the app for your chosen duration (5 min to 2 hours).

Can I restore my purchase?

Yes. Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your "Remove Ads" purchase on any device with the same Apple ID.

Why does the app need Screen Time permission?

Preyer uses Apple's Screen Time API to show a prayer before you open selected apps. This is the only way to intercept app launches on iOS, and your data stays private on your device.

Does Preyer collect my data?

No. All your data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
